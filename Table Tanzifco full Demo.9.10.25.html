More About Our Company

Tanzifco was established in 1963 in Kuwait. Today, as a strong regional player offering soft services, our operations span across the United Arab Emirates, Bahrain, Qatar, Oman, Egypt, India, Nepal and Sri Lanka as well. We provide all types of major and minor cleaning services besides offering a wide spectrum of specialized soft services. In the last 60 years, since 1963, Tanzifco has carved a niche for itself due to a highly professional work culture based on rigorous work ethics.
