This is a Grocerry Shop

A grocery store is a retail establishment that sells food and household items, ranging from fresh produce to packaged goods. While the term "grocery store" is often used interchangeably with "supermarket" in some regions, particularly the US, "supermarket" refers to a larger store with a broader selection of both food and non-food products. Smaller, more specialized grocery stores can include "convenience stores" for essentials, "greengrocers" for fruits and vegetables, and "delicatessens" for prepared foods.
